About Pacific Trails

Located on a breathtaking stretch of California coastline, Pacific Trails Resort was born from a lifelong passion for nature.

I'm Maggie, the owner and manager of Pacific Trails Resort. I have always found solace in the great outdoors--whether hiking through towering redwoods, listening to the soothing sound of ocean waves, or observing wildlife. I long dreamed of creating a sanctuary where guests could simultaneously enjoy nature along with the comforts of a luxury resort.

Pacific Trails is the fulfillment of that dream. Guests stay in yurts which were designed to harmoniously blend with the surrounding landscape and which offer stunning ocean views. In addition to our thoughtfully designed accommodations, we provide unique outdoor experiences that foster a deeper connection to the natural environment. Join us for guided hikes and bird watching trips, or rent a kayak for an immersive outdoor experience. We can't wait to share our coastal paradise with you!
